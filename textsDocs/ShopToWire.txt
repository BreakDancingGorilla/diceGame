Shop Buying Stats & Items 
-------------------------

buySelected Btn
    buy all selected stats and items 

selectedBuyTotal Btn
    total price of all selected stats and items

Items 
-----
reRollCost H1
    cost of the reRoll Token 

reRollSelectedAmt H1 
    amt of reRolls selected to buy.

reRollSelectUp Btn
    increments amt to buy

reRollSelectDown Btn 
    deincrement amt to buy


weightedDiceCost H1
    cost of the weighted Dice

weightedDiceSelectedAmt H1 
    amt of weighted Dice selected to buy.

weightedDiceSelectUp Btn
    increments amt to buy

weightedDiceSelectDown Btn 
    deincrement amt to buy

Shop Selling Dice 
-----------------

sellDice Btn
    Sell selected dice

dieSelectedAmt 1-6 H1
    Amt selected to sell. 

shopDieSelectUp 1-6 Btn
    increment amt to sell. 

shopDieSelectDown 1-6 Btn
    deincrement amt to sell. 

dieSellPrice 1-6 H1
    value to sell per unit.
